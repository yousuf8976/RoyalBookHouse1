Q/A Session

01

What kind of books do you help authors publish?

+
We work with authors across a wide range of genres and formats. Whether you’re writing fiction—such as romance, thrillers, or fantasy—or nonfiction, including memoirs, business, self-help, or educational content, we’ve got you covered. We also specialize in children’s books, poetry, art books, cookbooks, and more. From print to eBook and even audiobooks, we help bring your vision to life and ensure your book reaches the right audience on the right platforms.
02

What’s the typical timeline for publishing a book?

+
The publishing timeline can vary depending on the services you choose and the complexity of your book. On average, the process takes 4 to 12 weeks from manuscript submission to final publication. This includes editing, formatting, cover design, and distribution. If you're opting for advanced services like illustrations, marketing, or audiobook production, the timeline may extend slightly. Our team works efficiently to ensure high-quality results while keeping your launch on track.
03

Can I get my book edited before publishing with you?

+
Absolutely. We provide full editing support as part of our publishing process. From big-picture revisions to detailed proofreading, our editors ensure your manuscript meets the highest standards. Whether you need help with structure, flow, or final polish, we’ve got you covered.
04

Will I retain the rights to my book after publishing?

+
Yes — you retain full rights to your book. We believe authors should maintain complete ownership of their work. When you publish with us, you keep all intellectual property rights, including content, title, and royalties. Our goal is to support your publishing journey without taking control of your creative or legal ownership. You're free to market, sell, or republish your book however you choose.
05

What format should I use to submit my manuscript?

+
We recommend submitting your manuscript in Microsoft Word (.doc or .docx) or Google Docs format, as these are ideal for editing and formatting. You may also submit in PDF for review purposes, but editable formats are preferred to ensure smooth processing. Make sure your manuscript is in a single file, properly organized, and free of unnecessary formatting. If you have special elements like images, tables, or footnotes, please include those clearly marked or in a separate folder if needed.
06

How much does it cost to publish a book?

+
The cost of publishing a book can vary depending on the services you choose and the complexity of your project. Our publishing packages typically range from affordable basic plans to comprehensive premium services, which may include editing, design, ISBN registration, distribution, and marketing. We offer customized solutions to fit different budgets and goals, ensuring you get the support you need without overpaying. For an accurate quote, we recommend scheduling a free consultation so we can assess your specific publishing needs.
07

Do you offer marketing services for authors?

+
Yes, we provide a range of book marketing services designed to increase visibility, generate buzz, and drive sales. Our offerings include social media promotion, press releases, Amazon optimization (SEO and categories), email campaigns, author website creation, and more. Whether you’re launching a debut title or building long-term brand awareness, our marketing strategies are tailored to your book, genre, and target audience. We’re here to help you stand out in a crowded market.
08

Which platforms will my book be available on?

+
Your book will be distributed to a wide range of major platforms to ensure maximum visibility and global reach. These include Amazon (Kindle and Print), Barnes & Noble, Apple Books, Google Play Books, Kobo, IngramSpark, and Blurb, among others. Whether you're publishing an eBook, paperback, hardcover, or audiobook, we ensure your title is accessible across all leading marketplaces and devices worldwide.
09

Can I order printed copies of my book?

+
Yes, you can absolutely order printed copies of your book. We offer high-quality printing services with a variety of options, including paper type, trim size, binding styles, and cover finishes. Whether you need a small batch for personal use or a larger quantity for retail, events, or giveaways, we’ll help you choose the best print solution to match your goals and budget. You can also reorder copies at any time, with no minimum requirements.
10

Do you provide ISBNs for published books?

+
Yes, we provide ISBNs (International Standard Book Numbers) as part of our publishing packages. An ISBN is essential for identifying and distributing your book across major retailers and platforms. If you prefer, you may also use your own ISBN. Either way, we ensure your book is properly registered and ready for both print and digital distribution with professional metadata setup.
